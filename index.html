About College

St. Joseph's College of Engineering, established in 1994, is an autonomous institute located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, affiliated to Anna University, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NBA and NAAC. The college has achieved 1541 ranks including 57 Gold medals and 55 Silver medals, and has been continuously ranked by NIRF since 2017. Accredited with an A+ grade by securing 3.46 on a scale of 4.00 by NAAC, the college has completed 45 sponsored research projects worth Rs. 2.83 Crores funded by AICTE, NRB, DST, SERB, DBT, NRDMS, TNSCST, and Anna University. Recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (SIRO) since 2014 by DSIR, Government of India, the college has secured a 4.5-star rating for campus activities by the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) and has consistently received the 'National Employability Award' every year since 2017 by Aspiring Minds.

About Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering with state-of-art research facilities and well qualified faculty members produces young aspiring Engineers ever since its inception in 1994. The department is accredited by NBA and is recognized by Anna University as Nodal Centre for research since 2010 and thus offers Ph.D program for both part time and full time scholars. The department is well equipped with laboratories which include RF & Microwave Lab, Communication system lab, Signal processing lab, Networks lab, Optical communication lab, VLSI and Embedded lab. The department consistently strives for the overall development of the students by supporting them in all their endeavours through career development and placements activities. In order to keep pace with the latest technologies, our faculty enrich their knowledge through continuing education, technical consultancy, research & development. With this stride, the department has produced successful Electronics and Communication Engineers and has made them excel and flourish with the prominent and leading employers in this field. We have to our credit two MODROBS grants from AICTE and also a funded project from ISRO.

About Conference

'The International Conference on NextGen Engineering Technologies and Applications for Sustainable Development' (ICNEXTS'25) is a prestigious global event organized by department of ECE, St. Joseph's College of Engineering, Chennai. The primary aim is to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry professionals across the world.

With a strong focus on next-generation technologies, the conference covers topics including 5G/6G communication systems, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Robotics, Quantum Computing, Cybersecurity, VLSI, Nanotechnology, and Renewable Energy Systems. These emerging fields address global challenges such as environmental sustainability and smart infrastructure.

ICNEXTS'25 provides a dynamic platform for professionals and young researchers to present original work, explore collaborative opportunities, and gain insights into latest trends. The conference promotes quality research aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals, encouraging practical solutions for real-world problems.
