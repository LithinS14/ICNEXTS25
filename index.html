About College

St. Joseph's College of Engineering, established in 1994, is an autonomous institute located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The college is affiliated to Anna University, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NBA and NAAC. We have achieved 1541 ranks including 57 Gold medals and 55 Silver medals.

Continuously being ranked in NIRF since 2017. Accredited with A+ grade by securing 3.46 on a scale of 4.00 by NAAC. We have 45 Sponsored Research projects worth Rs. 2.83 Crores funded by AICTE, NRB, DST, SERB, DBT, NRDMS, TNSCST & Anna University.

Recognized as 'Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (SIRO)' since 2014 by DSIR, Government of India. College has secured 4.5-star rating for activities carried out in the campus by Institution's Innovation Council (IIC). Consistently awarded "National Employability Award" every year since 2017 by Aspiring Minds.

About Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering with state-of-the-art research facilities and well-qualified faculty members produces young aspiring Engineers ever since its inception in 1994. The department is accredited by NBA and offers PG (M.E Applied Electronics) under Anna University, Chennai.

The department is recognized by Anna University as Nodal Centre for research since 2010 and offers Ph.D program for both part-time and full-time scholars. We are well equipped with laboratories including RF & Microwave Lab, Communication System Lab, Signal Processing Lab, Networks Lab, Optical Communication Lab, VLSI and Embedded Lab.

The department consistently strives for overall development of students through career development and placement activities. Our faculty enrich their knowledge through continuing education, technical consultancy, and R&D. We have produced successful Electronics and Communication Engineers with prominent employers. We have two MODROBS grants from AICTE and a funded project from ISRO.

About Conference

The International Conference on NextGen Engineering Technologies and Applications for Sustainable Development (ICNEXTS'25) is a prestigious global event organized by St. Joseph's College of Engineering, Chennai. The primary aim is to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers from across the world.

With a strong focus on next-generation technologies, the conference covers topics including 5G/6G communication systems, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Robotics, Quantum Computing, Cybersecurity, VLSI, Nanotechnology, and Renewable Energy Systems. These emerging fields address global challenges such as environmental sustainability and smart infrastructure.

ICNEXTS'25 provides a dynamic platform for professionals and young researchers to present original work, explore collaborative opportunities, and gain insights into latest trends. The conference promotes quality research aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals, encouraging practical solutions for real-world problems.
