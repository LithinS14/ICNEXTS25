General Instructions

Guidelines for paper submission and conference participation

Submission Requirements

Submission of Paper

  • Authors are required to submit their paper adhering to the specified format.Click here for link
  • The final paper should be written in English with a maximum paper length of eight printed pages including figures,strictly with the IEEE manuscript template.
  • The submitted paper must be original and unpublished.
  • Double-blind peer review will be followed.
  • Please select relevant topics from the track areas.
  • Submissions, refereeing, and all correspondence will be done through e-mail(icnexts25@gmail.com).

Registration Details

  • At least one author must register to include the paper in the conference. If more than one author of an accepted paper wish to attend the conference, all such authors need to register separately as listener by paying applicable registration fee.
  • Any accepted paper included in the final program is expected to have at least one author attend and present the paper at the conference. Non-presented paper will not be included in the Journals specified below.
  • All the presented papers will be published in Google Scholar, Crossref
  • The decision of the review committee regarding the selection of paper will be final and binding
  • Individual Certificate will be provided for all authors
  • Author can present their work via hybrid mode if needed
  • Best paper in each category(Faculty/Industry/Professionals/Research Scholars/Students) will receive an appreciation E-Certificate
  • Extended version of selected paper will be published in Scopus indexed journals at an additional cost
  • Each registration covers only one paper per registrant. Each additional accepted paper can be registered as per the category of authors.
  • No refunds will be made after payment.
