General Instructions

Guidelines for paper submission and conference participation

Submission Requirements

Submission of Papers

  • Authors are required to submit their papers adhering to the specified format.click here for link
  • The final paper should be written in English with a maximum paper length of eight printed pages including figures strictly with the IEEE manuscript template.
  • The submitted papers must be original and unpublished.
  • Double-blind peer reviews will be followed.
  • All accepted papers will be forwarded to IEEE Explorer and extended version of paper will be published in the web of science /Scopus indexed journals.
  • Any accepted paper included in the final program is expected to have at least one author attend and present the paper at the conference. Non-presented paper will not be submitted to IEEE Xplore® digital library as per IEEE no-show policy.
  • Please select relevant topics from the topic areas.
  • Submissions, refereeing, and all correspondence will be conducted by e-mail.
  • NOTE: Please create your account of easychair.com if you don’t have.

Registration Details

  • At least one author must register to include the paper in the conference. If more than one author of an accepted paper wish to attend the conference, all such authors need to register separately as listener by paying applicable registration fee.
  • Certification Available (hardcopy/ softcopy ) only for paid Registration.
  • Each registration(IEEE member/non-member/student/Life Member) covers only one paper per registrant. Each additional accepted paper can be registered at applicable as per the category of authors.
  • No refunds will be made after payment.
  • NOTE: IETE members will receive a 50% refund after their paper is published.
